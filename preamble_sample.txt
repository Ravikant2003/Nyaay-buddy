This case summary outlines a pivotal legal challenge filed by Mr. Rajiv Mehta against the Union of India, contesting the constitutionality of Section 66A of the Information Technology Act, 2000. Initially adjudicated in the Delhi High Court and now escalated to the Supreme Court, the proceedings raise significant constitutional questions surrounding free speech and digital rights in India. The matter involves key legal precedents, expert testimony, and a broader implication on cyber law enforcement and civil liberties.
